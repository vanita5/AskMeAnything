How can I ask you a question?

Go to Ask, enter your question and click on the button. Simple as that.


Okay, but what's with your answer?

Well, there is a Twitter Bot that is going to send me the question via Twitter. As soon as I reply to the question, you can see the answer under Answers. And of course you can follow the Twitter bot and me, to see the answerS directly in your timeline.


I can't send you a question

Do you get an error? There could be several reasons for this. Either the bot or the webiste is rate limited. Only one question can be asked every minute to prevent spam. So if you get a rate limit error, you or another user has just asked a question. Please be patient and try again after a minute.
Not a rate limit error? Well, I guess then there's something wrong with Twitter. Or your input. Whatever.
